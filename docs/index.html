More than a URL cleaner

Six tools that make browsing better, built into one lightweight extension.

🧹

Tracker Removal

Strips utm_*, fbclid, gclid, and dozens more tracking parameters automatically from every URL.

🔀

Smart Redirects

Custom redirect rules to skip amp pages, paywalls, or route through preferred mirrors.

⚠️

Source Credibility

Powered by the CRED-1 dataset. Get warnings when visiting domains with low credibility scores.

🔧

Browser Tweaks

Re-enable text selection, disable autoplay videos, remove overlay popups, and fix broken right-click.

📦

Web Archive

Quick access to cached versions of pages via Wayback Machine and Google Cache.

🔒

Zero Data Collection

Everything runs on-device. No analytics, no telemetry, no accounts. Your browsing stays yours.
